About Me

I am a colorblind visual communication design major who used to be an architecture major. In other words, I draw/make pictures and patterns by hand and on the computer for myself and other people. I start with sketching a few concepts or ideas on paper, then choose the one I like best to refine on a computer. If I start on a computer then I use black and white for the initial idea to get a better idea of how it will look. After the first few steps it is reviewing and editing until I like how it looks.
